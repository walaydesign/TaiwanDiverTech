職業安全衛生承諾

HSE Promise

At our company, HSE considerations are our top priority. Before each operation, we conduct a Toolbox Meeting to promote open communication and ensure that all personnel are fully aware of the tasks at hand, including any associated risks. We also conduct periodic training to enhance our employees' knowledge of safety protocols and operational competencies, including operational procedures, maintenance practices, first aid, and emergency response.
Given the high-risk exposure faced by commercial divers, our supervisors are highly experienced in identifying and mitigating risks through the implementation of a Risk Assessment Management System (RAMS). This includes isolating all energy sources via properly designed Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedures to ensure that operations are carried out safely.
In the unlikely event of an accident, we have established a comprehensive emergency response plan to swiftly manage any risks and protect our personnel.

職業安全衛生（HSE）是公司的首要任務。
我們定期提供課程培訓，強化員工的安全知識和操作技能，涵蓋作業與維修流程、急救措施及應變處理，並在每次施工前都會進行「工具箱會議」，確保所有人員了解任務和風險。
鑒於商業潛水員所面臨的高風險及預防事故的發生，我們透過「風險評估管理系統」來識別和降低風險，並確實進行施工前的「上鎖掛牌程序」，以確保施工安全。
如不幸發生事故，我們有完善的應變計畫，來迅速應對和處理，保護我們的員工。
